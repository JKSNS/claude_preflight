# RISKS

Known risks. Tier 1+ projects maintain this; tier 2+ projects also maintain `THREAT_MODEL.md`.

## Format

```
## <NNN> — <short title>

Category:    security | reliability | data | financial | dependency | operational | agent-behavior
Description: <one paragraph: what is the risk?>
Likelihood:  low | medium | high
Impact:      low | medium | high | critical
Mitigation:  <what is in place that reduces this risk>
Residual:    <what remains uncovered>
Owner:       <human or "unowned">
Last reviewed: <YYYY-MM-DD>
```
